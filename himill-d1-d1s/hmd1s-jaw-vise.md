## 3. Usage Method

Rotate the handle to open the jaws, insert workpieces of different sizes; rotate the handle again to secure the workpiece.

<img src="/eng/himill-d1-d1s/media/hmd1s-jaw-vise/3-固定黄杨木.gif" alt="Secure Boxwood" style="width: 500px; height: auto; margin-bottom: 8px; border-radius: 16px;" />

<img src="/eng/himill-d1-d1s/media/hmd1s-jaw-vise/4-固定代木.gif" alt="Secure Substitute Wood" style="width: 500px; height: auto; margin-bottom: 8px; border-radius: 16px;" />

> ⚠️ **Usage Precautions**:
> 
> - Before clamping the workpiece, clean the jaws thoroughly
> - The jaw vise itself has a certain height. If the total height of the object being processed is too high, the spindle needs to be raised. Please raise it correctly according to the proper method
> - When securing metal workpieces, it is recommended to use the wrench, insert it into the hole of the handle to assist rotation, tighten as much as possible. And try the workpiece's firmness by hand
> 
> {.is-warning}
