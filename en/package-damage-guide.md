# Unboxing Precautions

## After-Sales Unboxing Guide

> 🚨 **<span style="color: #dc2626; font-size: 16px;">IMPORTANT: Please Read Before Unboxing</span>**
> 
> 1. **Please record an unboxing video immediately upon receiving the package.** This helps us verify any potential shipping damage.
> 2. **After opening the outer packaging, check if the plastic bag wrapping the equipment is damaged.**
> 3. **If any damage is found, please take photos of the damaged outer box** (such as dents, holes) **and the damaged plastic bag** wrapping the equipment.
> 4. **Failure to provide the above video and photos will result in us being unable to provide after-sales compensation service.** Thank you for your understanding and support.
> 
> {.is-danger}

If you receive a Maxmake product package and find any damage, please follow the process below to ensure you receive timely and effective compensation.

### 1. Contact Us Immediately
If there is any damage within 4 working days after signing for the package, you must contact us immediately via Discord or email so we can file a claim with the shipping company.

> ⚠️ **Important Notice**: If you contact us after 4 working days, there is a high probability that we will not be able to guarantee compensation.

#### Contact Methods
- <a href="https://discord.com/channels/1436232802227064903/1436232803061600351" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Discord</a>
- Email: support@maxmake.com

### 2. Preserve the Scene
Until the claim is settled, you must keep the outer box and all packaging materials. Do not discard them or attempt repairs yourself.

### 3. Complete Documentation
Please ensure you provide all the following evidence materials. Each one is essential:

#### 1. Outer Box with Shipping Label
The shipping label must be clearly visible.
