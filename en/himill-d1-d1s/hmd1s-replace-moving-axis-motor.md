# HiMill D1/D1S Moving Axis Motor Replacement Guide: XYZ Motor Swap Tutorial

Learn how to replace X, Y, and Z axis motors on [MAXMAKE HiMill D1/D1S](https://www.maxmake.com/products) CNC machine. This guide covers safety precautions, motor removal steps, and installation procedures for each axis.

---

## Safety Precautions

> ⚠️ **🔒 Safety Operation Guidelines**
> - Disconnect power during disassembly to prevent electric shock
> - For all screws, use the short side of the wrench to loosen first, then the long side to prevent stripping
> - Save removed screws in order for easy reinstallation, take photos if possible
> {.is-warning}
