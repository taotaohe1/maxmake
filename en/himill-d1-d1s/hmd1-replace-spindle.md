# HiMill D1 Spindle Replacement Guide: Motor, Board & Fan Swap Tutorial

Learn how to replace spindle components in HiMill D1 CNC machine. This guide covers spindle replacement, rear circuit board replacement, cooling fan replacement, and spindle light replacement.

---

## Safety Precautions

> ⚠️ **🔒 Safety Operation Guidelines**
> - Disconnect power during disassembly to prevent electric shock
> - For all screws, use the short side of the wrench to loosen first, then the long side to prevent stripping
> - Save removed screws in order for easy reinstallation, take photos if possible
> {.is-warning}
