# HiMill D1/D1S Button & Indicator Replacement Guide: LED & Switch Swap

Learn how to replace external and internal buttons and indicators on HiMill D1/D1S CNC machine. This guide covers safety precautions, component removal, and installation steps for control interface repair.

---

## Safety Precautions

> ⚠️ **🔒 Safety Operation Guidelines**
> - Disconnect power during disassembly to prevent electric shock
> - For all screws, use the short side of the wrench to loosen first, then the long side to prevent stripping
> - Save removed screws in order for easy reinstallation, take photos if possible
> {.is-warning}
