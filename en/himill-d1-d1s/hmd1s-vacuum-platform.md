## 7. Notes

> ⚠️ **Safety Reminder**
> - After processing is complete, please turn off the vacuum air pump first, wait for 1~2 minutes, then remove the workpiece and clean the vacuum platform in time
> - If a large amount of debris is inhaled, please turn off the power immediately, clean or replace the filter element
> {.is-warning}

> 💡 **Usage Tips**
> - Excess rubber strips can be cut off. If not cut off, please insert the excess part into the groove to avoid affecting suction when placed on the table
> - Rubber strips cannot be cross-stacked, otherwise it may leak air and affect suction
> - At the interface of the rubber strips, try to fit them as closely as possible to avoid gaps, otherwise it may leak air and affect suction
> {.is-warning}
