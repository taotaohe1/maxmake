## Spindle Collet Cleaning

Regularly cleaning the spindle collet can maintain its cleanliness, ensure tool holding stability, and extend the collet's service life.

<a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kU1k7iBTnJY" target="_blank">Click to Watch Video</a>

### Cleaning Steps

**Step 1: Remove the Collet**

1. First, pull down the lever to release the collet's fixation
2. Use a wrench to remove the collet from the spindle. Be sure to save the collet components properly.

**Step 2: Clean the Collet Gaps**

Carefully use a craft knife or zip tie to clean dust and debris from the collet gaps, ensuring the gaps are clean without any residue.

**Step 3: Clean the Collet Interior**

Use cleaning tools to clean the inside of the collet, removing accumulated dust and debris to keep the collet's inner wall clean.

> 💡 **Maintenance Tips**:
> - Regularly clean the collet to maintain optimal working condition
> - Ensure the collet is firmly installed when reinstalling
> - Replace the collet immediately if any damage is found
> {.is-info}
