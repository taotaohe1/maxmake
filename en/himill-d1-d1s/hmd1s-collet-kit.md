## 4. Precautions

> ⚠️ **Safety Reminder**
> - When installing and removing the collet, ensure the machine has completely stopped operating
> - Use appropriate wrench force to avoid overtightening and damaging threads
> - Regularly check collet wear and replace promptly
> - After tool installation, check clamping tightness
> - Reduction sleeve and collet must fit completely to avoid looseness
> {.is-warning}

> 💡 **Usage Tips**
> - 6mm collet is specifically for 6mm diameter tools, cannot be used for other sizes
> - Keep collet clean, regularly remove chips and oil
> - When not in use for long periods, remove collet and store properly
> - Reduction sleeve can adapt to smaller diameter tools, expanding usage range
> {.is-warning}
