# Side Clamp

## 1. Parts List

- ✅ Fixing screws × Several
- ✅ Wrench × 1
- ✅ Side clamp × 2

Parts contents are as follows:

<img src="/eng/himill-d1-d1s/media/hmd1s-side-clamp/1-展示配件.gif" alt="Accessories Display" style="width: 600px; height: auto; margin-bottom: 8px; border-radius: 16px;" />

## 2. Clamp Face Adjustment

Use the wrench from the accessories box to loosen the screw and raise the clamp face.

<img src="/eng/himill-d1-d1s/media/hmd1s-side-clamp/2-钳面上升.gif" alt="Clamp Face Raised" style="width: 600px; height: auto; margin-bottom: 8px; border-radius: 16px;" />

## 3. Secure Side Clamp

Take out the fixing screws from the accessories box and secure the side clamp to the work surface.

<img src="/eng/himill-d1-d1s/media/hmd1s-side-clamp/3-固定侧推钳.gif" alt="Secure Side Clamp" style="width: 600px; height: auto; margin-bottom: 8px; border-radius: 16px;" />

## 4. Secure Workpiece

Tighten the upper screw to push the clamp face forward and secure the workpiece.

<img src="/eng/himill-d1-d1s/media/hmd1s-side-clamp/4-固定工件.gif" alt="Secure Workpiece" style="width: 600px; height: auto; margin-bottom: 8px; border-radius: 16px;" />

> ⚠️ **Usage Notes**:
> 
> - Ensure the work surface is clean before installation
> - When securing the workpiece, apply moderate force to avoid damaging the workpiece
> - Before use, check that the side clamp is securely fixed to the work surface
>
